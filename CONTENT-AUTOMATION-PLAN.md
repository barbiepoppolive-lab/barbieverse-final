## 2. CONTENT PILLARS (What We Post)

### Pillar 1: Earning Proof (35% of content)
**Why**: Social proof is #1 converter in India. People need to SEE money before they believe.

**Content types**:
- Weekly earnings screenshot carousel ("This week: ₹4,200 from Poppo")
- Payment proof posts (bank screenshot, blurred sensitive data)
- "How I earned ₹X this month" breakdown carousel
- Before/after income growth (month 1 vs month 3)
- "Top earner of the week" celebration post

**Emotional trigger**: FOMO + aspiration

### Pillar 2: How-To Education (30% of content)
**Why**: New streamers don't know where to start. We become their go-to resource.

**Content types**:
- "How to start live streaming on Poppo" (step-by-step carousel)
- "5 mistakes new streamers make" (carousel)
- "Lighting setup under ₹500" (reel)
- "How to talk to viewers" (reel with tips)
- "PK battle strategy guide" (carousel)
- "Best time to stream in India" (infographic)

**Emotional trigger**: Empowerment + clarity

### Pillar 3: Streamer Lifestyle (25% of content)
**Why**: Aspiration sells. People want to see what their life could look like.

**Content types**:
- "Day in the life of a Poppo streamer" (reel)
- "My streaming setup tour" (reel)
- "Morning routine before going live" (story)
- "What I wear for streams" (carousel with outfit ideas)
- "My desk setup for streaming" (carousel)
- Motivational quotes with Barbie character

**Emotional trigger**: Aspiration + relatability

### Pillar 4: Coin Selling & Recharge (15% of content)
**Why**: Coins are how streamers earn. Viewers buy coins → send gifts → streamers get diamonds → convert to cash. Our agency sells coins at cheaper rates than in-app (avoid 30% Apple/Google tax). This is a direct revenue stream.

**How the coin economy works**:
1. Viewer buys coins (₹100 in-app = ₹70 after Apple/Google tax)
2. Viewer sends gift to streamer (e.g., "Rose" = 5 coins)
3. Streamer receives diamonds (converted from gifts)
4. Streamer cashes out diamonds to bank account
5. Agency earns commission on streamer's earnings

**Content types**:
- "How to buy Poppo coins cheaper" (carousel — price comparison)
- "Why buying through agency saves you 30%" (carousel)
- "Step-by-step: How to recharge coins" (reel with screen recording)
- "What can you do with coins?" (carousel — gifts, VIP, events)
- "Coin vs Diamond: What's the difference?" (infographic)
- "Top gifts and what they cost" (carousel)
- "How to get free coins" (story — daily tasks, events)
- "Coin recharge proof" (story — instant delivery screenshots)
- "₹100 in-app vs ₹70 through us" (reel — price comparison)

**Emotional trigger**: Trust + savings + convenience

**Trust-building content** (CRITICAL for India — coin scams are rampant):
- "How to verify a legitimate agency" (carousel)
- "BarbieVerse is verified by Poppo" (story with proof)
- "Instant delivery guarantee" (reel)
- "24/7 WhatsApp support" (story)
- Real customer testimonials (carousel)
- "What happens if coins don't arrive?" (FAQ carousel)

**Coin selling content calendar**:
- Monday: Price comparison carousel (in-app vs agency)
- Wednesday: How-to recharge reel
- Friday: Trust signal / testimonial

### Pillar 5: Community & Engagement (10% of content)
**Why**: Engagement signals boost algorithm reach. We need comments, shares, saves.

**Content types**:

- Questions: "What's stopping you from going live?"
- "Streamer spotlight" — feature a successful member
- Q&A sessions in stories
- "Guess the earnings" game post
- Celebration posts for milestones

**Emotional trigger**: Belonging + participation

## 3. PLATFORM-SPECIFIC STRATEGY

### Instagram (PRIMARY — 5 posts/week)

**Why Instagram first**: Highest engagement for visual content, Reels algorithm favors new accounts, carousels get 3x more saves than single images.

**Content format mix**:
| Format | Frequency | Purpose |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| Carousels | 3/week | Education + Earning Proof |
| Reels | 2/week | Lifestyle + Quick Tips |
| Stories | Daily | Polls, Questions, BTS |
| Static Posts | 1/week | Motivation + Community |

**Carousel Strategy (THE KING FORMAT)**:
- **Slides**: 5-7 (sweet spot for saves)
- **Dimensions**: 1080x1350px (4:5 portrait — 33% more feed space)
- **Structure**: Hook → Problem → Insight × N → CTA
- **Slide 1**: Bold hook, NOT a title ("Stop making this streaming mistake")
- **Slide 2**: Promise slide ("In this carousel: 5 things top streamers do differently")
- **Slides 3-6**: One insight per slide, visual + text
- **Final slide**: CTA ("Join BarbieVerse — link in bio")

**Reels Strategy**:
- **Length**: 15-30 seconds (optimal for completion rate)
- **Hook**: First 1-3 seconds must stop scroll
- **Text overlay**: Always (85% watch on mute)
- **Format**: Talking head, screen recordings, quick tips
- **Audio**: Trending sounds when relevant

**Posting Times (IST)**:
- Carousels: 9:00 AM (morning commute scroll)
- Reels: 12:00 PM or 6:00 PM (lunch/evening)
- Stories: 7:00 PM (evening engagement)

**Hashtag Strategy** (20-25 per post):
```
Primary: #BarbieVerse #PoppoLive #VoneLive #LiveStreaming #EarnFromHome
Niche: #StreamerLife #LiveHost #GoLive #IndianStreamer #PoppoAgency
Trending: #WorkFromHome #SideHustleIndia #OnlineEarning #ReelsIndia
Location: #India #Mumbai #Delhi #Bangalore
```

### Facebook (SECONDARY — 3 posts/week)

**Why Facebook**: Largest user base (3B MAU), Groups get stronger reach than Pages, older demographic (25-45) has more disposable income.

**Content format mix**:
| Format | Frequency | Purpose |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| Reels | 2/week | Repurpose Instagram Reels |
| Image Posts | 1/week | Earning proof, testimonials |
| Group Posts | Daily | Community engagement |
| Live Q&A | 1/week | Direct engagement |

**Facebook-Specific Tactics**:
- **Create a Group**: "BarbieVerse Streamers" — Groups get 10x more reach than Pages
- **Cross-post Instagram Reels** (but re-upload natively, don't share link)
- **Post native video** (Facebook suppresses outbound links)
- **Engage in comments** within first 30 minutes (algorithm signal)
- **Use Facebook Reels** — algorithm actively pushes them to non-followers

**Posting Times (IST)**:
- Reels: 12:00 PM
- Image posts: 7:00 PM
- Group posts: 9:00 AM

**Hashtag Strategy** (3-5 per post):
```
#PoppoLive #LiveStreaming #EarnFromHome #BarbieVerse
```

### YouTube Shorts (TERTIARY — 3/week)

**Why YouTube**: Search-driven discovery (people search "how to earn from Poppo"), long-term evergreen content, Shorts algorithm favors new channels.

**Content format mix**:
| Format | Frequency | Purpose |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| Shorts | 3/week | Quick tips, earning proof |
| Long-form | 1/month | Deep tutorials (future) |

**Shorts Strategy**:

- **Hook**: First 1-3 seconds (no intro, no pleasantries)
- **Format**: "Did you know?", "One tip that changed everything", "Mistake reveal"
- **CTA**: "Subscribe for more streaming tips"

**Posting Times (IST)**:

**Hashtags**:
